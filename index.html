our story

ABOUT US

This fitness club is one of the recognized fitness club brands in India. In the year 2015, the brand was establish with an aim to provide friendly, comfortable and well maintained fitness club with a aim to bridge the gap between the premium clubs and neighbourhood’s unorganized clubs and give a massive superior facility at a feasible prices.!

HIGH QUALITY EXERCISE EQUIPMENTS

Gym equipment is an integral part of any fitness facility and we ensure up to date equipments ,intuitive as well as new equipments to our members.

MEMBER AMENITIES AND FACILTIES

we provide our members amenities and privileges like family guest pass, juice bar and even childcare services. We offer different package levels that cater to various target customers.

WELL DESIGNED SPACE AND FACILITY

We pay strong attention to design trends that align closely with the well-being policies of the modern world. our gym interior emphasises and supports the many strands of environmental and human ethics, as well as the global pursuit of good health and happiness. to maximise user-satisfaction we implement wellness-driven design strategies that results in correct use of space, lighting, colour schemes and material selection.
